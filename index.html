      📚 What to Read Before June
      Start Here · Foundational
      Black's Law Dictionary
      — The definitive legal reference
      Over 55,000 law-related terms. New paralegals should prioritize this first. Get familiar with the language before anything else — legal vocabulary is half the battle.
      Not Started
      Start Here · Foundational
      Introduction to Law for Paralegals
      Currier & Eimermann
      Covers the role of the paralegal, sources and classification of law, court system structure, and an overview of litigation. Basically the roadmap for everything you'll study.
      Not Started
      Level Up · Legal Writing
      The Redbook
      Bryan A. Garner
      Specifically designed for legal document preparation — grammar, punctuation, and style rules for legal writing. Legal writing is a whole different beast. You'll need this fast.
      Not Started
      Big Picture · Orientation
      Law 101
      Jay M. Feinman
      Plain-English overview of how American law actually works. Great for orientation before you dive into the technical material. Makes everything less intimidating.

      Hands-on skills and real-world context — identifying legal issues, breaking rules into elements, applying rules to facts. Practical from page one.
